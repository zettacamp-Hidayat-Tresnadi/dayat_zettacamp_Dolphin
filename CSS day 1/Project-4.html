Album: The Book

The Book is the debut extended play (EP) by Japanese duo Yoasobi. It was released through Sony Music Entertainment Japan on January 6, 2021, the same date with their single "Kaibutsu", accompanied the second season of Beastars, "Encore" serves as a promotional single of the EP.

Halzion

The song depicts the protagonist who spends days of disappointment after parting with her lover, finding hope while re-questioning her dreams and the meaning of life, and regains her motivation to move into the future.

Tabun

A middle tempo song, it depicts a couple breaking up in the morning. The song also accompanied the movie of the same name, Tabun, based on the same novel.
