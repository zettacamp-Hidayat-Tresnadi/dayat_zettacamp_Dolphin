Our Artist

Yoasobi

YOASOBI (stylized in all caps) is a Japanese music duo composed of vocaloid music producer Ayase and vocalist Ikura (幾田りら).

Minami Kuribayashi

Minami Kuribayashi is a Japanese singer, songwriter, lyricist and voice actress from Shizuoka Prefecture. From 2016 to 2019, she performed under the stage name Minami.

Aimer

Aimer is a Japanese pop singer and lyricist signed to Sacra Music and managed by Agehasprings. Aimer uses the name aimerrhythm for lyrics credits.
